# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2024-07-26

AI Fitness Coach ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Personal Information:** Name, email, age, height, weight, fitness goals.
- **Health Data:** Workout history, exercise types, repetitions, calorie burn, and other biometric data.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and camera access (for pose detection).

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Personalization:** To tailor workouts and provide personalized feedback.
- **Analytics:** To improve our App, services, and machine learning models.
- **Communication:** To send you updates and support messages.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized and aggregated data with third parties for research and analytics purposes.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion at any time.

## 5. Your Rights

- **GDPR:** You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
- **CCPA:** You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at [support@aifitness.coach](mailto:support@aifitness.coach).
